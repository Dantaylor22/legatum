This Privacy Policy explains how Digital Relative ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use our digital legacy vault service at digitalrelative.co.uk ("the Service").

3. How we use your data

We process your data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract performance — to deliver the vault service you have subscribed to
  • Legitimate interests — to maintain security, prevent fraud, and improve the service
  • Legal obligation — to comply with UK law, including financial regulations
  • Consent — for marketing communications (you may withdraw at any time)

4. Encryption and security

Your vault contents (credentials, passwords, notes) are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before they leave your device. The encryption key is derived from your password using PBKDF2 with 200,000 iterations. This means that neither Digital Relative, nor our infrastructure providers, can access the contents of your vault — only you and your nominated beneficiaries can.

We enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all email/password accounts. We conduct regular security reviews and store all data in EU-based data centres.

6. International transfers

Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the ICO.

7. How long we keep your data

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Vault contents are retained until you delete them or close your account. On account deletion, all data is permanently and irreversibly erased within 30 days. Anonymised usage statistics may be retained indefinitely.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data (Settings → Export data)
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — delete your account and all data (Settings → Delete account)
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON export)
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any right, contact privacy@digitalrelative.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. No cookie consent banner is required for essential cookies only.

10. Children

Our Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

We will notify you by email of any material changes to this policy. Continued use of the Service after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
